  • Automatic switch

    The automatic switch allows for more energy efficiency by controlling lighting based on motion sensing. Light comes ON only when and as long as it is needed. And it turns OFF automatically.

  • Time delay switch

    The time delay switch can be used to replace existing light switches for additional control and energy efficiency. Upon installation, a certain “time delay” is selected. The light will turn OFF automatically after this pre-determined period.

  • 2-gang switch with transparent rocker

    Transparent rockers can be used instead of standard rocker plates. They allow you to create your very own design – and change it whenever you wish.
